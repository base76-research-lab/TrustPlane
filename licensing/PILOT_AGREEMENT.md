## 1. Scope

Provider grants Client a 30-day non-exclusive evaluation access to TrustPlane (the "Pilot") under the following terms.

The Pilot includes:
- One (1) tenant provisioned in the TrustPlane SaaS environment
- API key and integration documentation
- Weekly signal report (D7, D14)
- Final PDF summary report (D30) covering request volume, PASS/ESCALATE/BLOCK distribution, and EU AI Act Article 9/12/13/14 mapping against Client's observed traffic

The Pilot does **not** include:
- SLA guarantees
- Custom policy configuration (available in paid tier)
- Production-grade uptime commitment

Client warrants that no special categories of personal data (GDPR Art. 9) will be transmitted through the Pilot environment unless explicitly agreed in writing.

Provider processes data as a Data Processor on behalf of Client (Data Controller). Full DPA terms are attached as Annex A.
